About the Editor

S.C. Thomas — Editor-in-Chief

Founder of NY Spotlight Report. Covering the intersection of AI, automation, and entrepreneurship from New York since 2020.

S.C. Thomas founded NY Spotlight Report in March 2020 as an independent digital publication dedicated to covering the rapidly evolving landscape of artificial intelligence, business automation, and digital entrepreneurship.

Based in New York, the publication operates at the intersection of technology and business, delivering actionable reporting on the tools, systems, and strategies that are reshaping how people build and run businesses in the modern economy.

Under Thomas's editorial direction, NY Spotlight Report has developed a fully autonomous publishing system powered by over 100 AI agents. This system manages everything from content research and drafting to distribution and audience engagement, serving as both a subject of coverage and a proof of concept for the automation strategies the publication reports on.

The publication reaches thousands of readers monthly across its website, newsletter, and syndication channels, with a focus on practical, experience-driven reporting that readers can act on immediately.
