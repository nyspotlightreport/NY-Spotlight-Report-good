Editor-in-Chief

S.C. Thomas

U.S. Military Veteran · Founder · Editor-in-Chief

S.C. Thomas is a U.S. Military Veteran and entertainment industry insider who founded NY Spotlight Report in 2020 to cover New York's entertainment, live performance, fashion, culture, and nightlife scenes from the inside.

Before launching the publication, Thomas spent years embedded in New York's entertainment world — not as a celebrity, but as someone who understood that the best stories come from staying in the shadows and being in the rooms where it happens. That philosophy defines every story NY Spotlight Report publishes.

The military instilled discipline, precision, and a commitment to truth that carries through to every piece of reporting. In a media landscape dominated by PR-approved press releases and paid placements, NY Spotlight Report operates differently: independent, unsponsored, and accountable only to the reader.

Under Thomas's leadership, the publication has grown from a solo operation into a digital newsroom covering Broadway, Off-Broadway, the underground music scene, New York Fashion Week, gallery openings, nightlife culture, and the people shaping the city's creative future.

Thomas brings a simple editorial standard: report what's real, from inside the room, without asking permission.

What We Cover

Entertainment

Broadway, Off-Broadway, film premieres, comedy, live events, and the business behind New York's entertainment industry.

Fashion

Culture
