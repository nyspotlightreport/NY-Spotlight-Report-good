Information and resources for journalists, podcasters, and media professionals covering AI, technology, and digital entrepreneurship.

NY Spotlight Report is an independent digital publication based in New York City, covering artificial intelligence, business automation, digital entrepreneurship, and the tools reshaping the modern economy.

Founded in March 2020 by S.C. Thomas, the publication delivers experience-driven reporting on AI systems, passive income strategies, content automation, and growth tactics. Our coverage is grounded in hands-on testing and real-world implementation, not press releases.

The publication operates on a fully autonomous publishing infrastructure powered by over 100 AI agents, making it both a reporting outlet and a live case study in the automation it covers.
