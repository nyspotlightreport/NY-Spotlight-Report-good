Send Us a Tip

Know something we should be covering? We investigate every credible lead and protect every source.

NY Spotlight Report relies on tips from insiders, industry professionals, performers, venue staff, and anyone who knows what's really happening in New York's entertainment, fashion, culture, and nightlife scenes.

We never reveal our sources. Your identity is protected by our editorial standards and our Editor-in-Chief's personal guarantee. If you ask to remain anonymous, you will.

When sending a tip, include as much context as possible: names, dates, locations, and any supporting documentation. The more specific you are, the faster we can investigate.

What we investigate:

Unreported stories in NYC entertainment, Broadway and Off-Broadway productions, fashion industry practices, nightlife venue operations, cultural institution decisions, event industry disputes, and anything involving public figures in New York's creative economy.

Our Promise to Sources

Your identity is sacred. We have never burned a source and we never will. This publication exists because people trust us with the truth.

Tips can also be submitted anonymously through any channel. You don't have to identify yourself for us to investigate a story.
